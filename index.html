About Me

Bhavana Dalvi is a Lead Research Scientist at AI2. Her research interests are in the area of Generative AI, Machine reasoning, and Explainability. She received her PhD from the Carnegie Mellon University in 2015 and M.Tech from the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay in 2007. Prior to her PhD, she worked at Google Bangalore for two years. Besides research, she loves hiking, meditation, and music.
